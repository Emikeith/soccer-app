Our History

Established in 2018, Alena Soccer Association began with a simple mission: to elevate the standard of amateur soccer in Winnipeg. What started as a 4-team casual league has evolved into a premier competitive community.

Community Impact

We bridge the gap between recreational play and professional organization. With sanctioned officiating and state-of-the-art facilities, we provide a platform for athletes to showcase their skills.

LEAGUE RULES

01

Code of Conduct

All players must respect officials, opponents, and staff. Zero tolerance for physical or verbal abuse. Automatic 3-match ban for red cards involving dissent.

02

Attendance & RSVP

Players must RSVP via the app 24 hours prior to kick-off. Teams failing to field 7 players at the 10-minute mark forfeit the match (3-0 loss).

03

Uniform Policy

Shin guards are mandatory. Teams must wear assigned jersey colors as indicated in the fixture list. Failure to comply results in a $10 kit rental fee.
