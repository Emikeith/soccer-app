The Legacy

Established in 2012, the Alena Soccer Association was founded with a vision to promote an active and healthy lifestyle through the love of soccer. What began as a small community of passionate players has grown into a welcoming association that brings people together both on and off the field.

Est. 2012

More than just a soccer league, Alena is a place where friendships are built, teamwork is celebrated, and new connections are formed. The association encourages players to socialize after games, creating a strong sense of community and belonging among members.

Alena Soccer Association is also dedicated to helping newcomers feel at home by providing opportunities to meet new people, build friendships, and become part of a supportive community through sport. Through soccer, the association continues to inspire healthy living, unity, and lasting relationships in Winnipeg.

LEAGUE RULES

01

Code of Conduct

All players must respect officials, opponents, and staff. Zero tolerance for physical or verbal abuse. Automatic 3-match ban for red cards involving dissent.

02

Attendance & RSVP

Players must RSVP via the app 24 hours prior to kick-off. Teams failing to field 7 players at the 10-minute mark forfeit the match (3-0 loss).

03

Uniform Policy

Shin guards are mandatory. Teams must wear assigned jersey colors as indicated in the fixture list. Failure to comply results in a $10 kit rental fee.
